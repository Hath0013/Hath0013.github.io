Canadian Science Publishing

CSP is Canada's largest independent, not-for-profit publisher of international peer-reviewed scientific journals, formerly known as NRC Research Press. On the Marketing team, I worked closely with Communications Specialists, Scientific Publishing Editors and subject matter experts to strengthen CSP's brand.

Key contributions to Canadian Science Publishing:

  1. Led scientific journal cover selection and production. Creating brand templates made the process with sales, journal editors and marketing more efficient.
  2. Initiated usability testing of sales and marketing tools. Conducted in-house user testing of current tools to either improve or replace current tools.
  1. Implemented tools with built-in metrics. This gave the team a way to measure success, report quantitative data, and inform data-driven decisions.
  2. Strengthened brand and visual presence across all channels. Brought design consistency and awareness to the brand. Librarians and agents have increased recognition of CSP since starting promotional campaigns.
