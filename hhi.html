Half Halt Illustrations

My Passion Project (2020-2023)

I started Half Halt Illustrations in March 2020 as a way to fuel my creative inspiration during the first pandemic lockdown in Ontario. As an active equestrian and horse owner, Half Halt Illustrations combined my passion for creativity and love of the equestrian sport.
