OpenCounter, a Euna Solutions Brand

Sales & Marketing Campaign | Permits

OVERVIEW

The goal of this campaign was to showcase how digital tools are essential to the efficiency of special events permitting, what specific features to look for in a digital system, and best practices for implementation.

OpenCounter (now Euna Permits) is a web-based budgeting software that is offered as both a hosted or SaaS model.

ROLE

Graphic Designer & Developer

Graphic Design, Illustration, Visual Design

Some examples of the final eBook spreads.

Results and takeaways

  • Work closely with project stakeholders. This helps clear up any questions that may arise before any design work starts, saving time and revisions later in the project timeline.
  • Retain as much negative space as possible. Negative space is key is balancing copy-heavy long-form content like this eBook.
  • Expand your design comfort zone. I enjoyed the challenge of varying design elements to elevate the content as much as possible here while staying on brand.
