UX Design

Snowbird Haven was in dire need of a solution to grow their business. Working with Snowbird Haven involved re-branding and updating their outdated website. This included the need for search engine optimization and an easily-accessible way for the target audience to view available accomodations.

Snowbird Haven is a quiet and beautiful resort that offers cottage and RV site rentals in White Lake, ON. Their primary focus is to accomodate citizens with long and short-term vacation rentals.

Objectives

Working with Snowbird Haven involved re-branding and building a stunning promotional business website. The new website was built to be easily-accessible and allowed clients to find information on long and short-term rentals in White Lake, ON.

Goals

  • Invest most of the time re-branding and designing the new website.
  • Examining and collecting data on competitors.
  • Structure information architecture to facilitate the users browsing behaviour.
  • Create a mobile-first, responsive site that works effortlessly on all desktop and mobile devices.
  • Include an easy way to view rental availability.
  • Include metadata tactics for improved search engine optimization.
  • Provide user-focused content, structure, and navigation.
  • Ensure ease of use for all site editors independent of their level of technology understanding.
