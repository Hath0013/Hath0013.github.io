Bonfire, a Euna Solutions Brand

Sales & Marketing Campaign | eProcurement

OVERVIEW

Bonfire releases a State of Public Sourcing Report (SOPS) annually. The 2023 report is backed by data collected between January 1 – May 31, 2023, from almost 600 U.S. public agencies that use Bonfire. The complex data needed to be easy to digest and follow a retrofuturism style.

Bonfire (now Euna Procure) is a cloud-based procurement software uses a SaaS model.

ROLE

Graphic Designer & Developer

User Research, Design Research, Advertising Creative, Visual Design, Landing Page Design

Results and takeaways

  • Research, research, research. I was already familiar with the retrofuturism theme, but to bring this campaign to life I needed to know it inside and out. I studied the history of the era as well as the design history through books, web articles—I even watched a few movies set in that time to get my mind into that space.
  • Good design drives results. Increased the landing page conversion rate by 5.5x the conversion rate for the previous year AND this report recorded the most downloads of all previous years.
  • Consistency is key. Building cohesiveness from page to page was critical in establishing a retrofuturism theme which translated into a beatifully designed campaign.
  • Overcome creative block. It really helped me to connect with my team and bounce ideas around whenever I got stuck on something.
