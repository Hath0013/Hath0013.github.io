About Hannah

With a background in photography and graphic design, I enjoy creating rich, interactive experiences for digital platforms and print media. My recent work includes front-end web design, user experience and interface design for virtual reality, and magazine layout design.

Working in the design industry for over four years has grown my experience in client communication, learning how to collaborate effectively with a team, and develop an efficient workflow.

This expertise has brought measurable success to the organizations I’ve had the opportunity to work with, and improved their products tenfold.
