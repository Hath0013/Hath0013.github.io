A little bit about myself.

My name is Hannah Hatherell and I'm passionate about improving the lives of others through design. I'm constantly learning new things everyday and love meeting people who are motivated and unafraid of sharing their ideas with the world. I’m currently based in Ottawa, Canada.

I am well versed and schooled in photography, graphic, web and interaction design. I’ve learnt some of the processes, concepts and theories of UX/UI through attending workshops, conferences, webinars and reading blogs in my spare time at work. I have a strong passion for web development and challenge myself to continue cultivating this passion. I am very much a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to development.

My values

📚 Always be learning.

Humans are constant works in progress that need to receive feedback in order to grow. I am always looking at ways to challenge and improve myself, which is why in my spare time I like to participate in online courses, read blogs or books on subjects in my field and dabble in new projects.

🤓 Work smarter, not harder.

I can’t count the amount of times I’ve found myself getting frustrated by repeating the same action multiple times when trying to achieve something. Constantly asking the question “is there a better way to do accomplish this” has opened me up to a world of automated process-learning, and I'll never go back.

🎨 Embrace your creativity.

Everyone needs a creative outlet of some sort - else, we'd go insane! When I'm not designing, I'm working on art in Procreate, commissions for my micro-business or exercising my film photography skills with my Pentax K1000.

😌 Stay humble.

It doesn't matter who you are, bottom line is to always treat others with respect as you would yourself. Because ultimately we're all human sharing the same wants and needs and facing our own battles everyday. Stay true to yourself and stay true to others.
