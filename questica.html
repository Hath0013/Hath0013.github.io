Questica, a Euna Solutions Brand (Budgeting Software)

Questica is one of the multiple product lines I provided Graphic Design support for under Euna Solutions. This ABM campaign was a fun project highlight. The campaign promoted a new product feature called Budget Book Studio. A focused approach targeted prospects, in this case we filtered by State/Province, to attain new leads. We utilized MailJoy for the direct mail marketing portion and designed 9 state/province specific postcards.

Design Parameters

  • Eye-catching. Visually engage with the viewer, they'll want to hang this in their office.
  • Memorable. Design something on-brand, but break out of the core corporate mold.
  • Print-ready. The CMYK graphics should be sharp as a tack.
  • Be familial. All the postcards should look like they belong together.
