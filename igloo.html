Igloo Software

As Visual Designer on the Professional Services team, I provide internal and customer support for digital workplace visual design and branding. This is achieved with customer consultation, brand analysis, design system builds, theming, implementation and testing. I strive for cross-functional collaboration to improve processes & platform features.

Key contributions to Igloo Software:

  1. Beautiful, accessibility-compliant design systems. Ensuring customer digital workplace themes are visually cohesive, reinforcing brand and visual consistency, and meet business goals and brand requirements.
  2. Custom CSS coding. Writing scalable, custom CSS classes and styling for digital workplace themes and widgets, to satisfy per-customer brand requirements, including inline HTML.
  3. Product & UX support. Collaborating with UX and Product teams through advocating for design improvements by providing customer insights to optimize platform usability.
  1. Excellent customer service. Strengthening Igloo's service reputation by fostering strong customer relationships through delivery of high-quality design solutions and support. Serving as a trusted Visual Design consultant for branded UI solutions and accessibility.
  2. Advanced visual design documentation & process systems. Creating reliable, scalable reference materials for both internal teams and customers, strengthening workflows, streamlining decision-making, and supporting long-term operational clarity.
