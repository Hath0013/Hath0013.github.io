MasterpieceVR is a 3D sculpting and painting tool that allows users to build tangible objects, colourful environments, and high quality models. It also allows you to sculpt with friends in the same room in real time, or invite spectators to watch while you create.

Image Source

On a team of three game developers and two UX designers, we were tasked with designing and building an annotation system for MasterpieceVR. This system allows users to add text to any object in virtual space.

We designed the interface and user experience for the annotation system. We made interactive prototypes in Adobe XD and the developed them in Unity. We also conducted user testing of the prototypes and gathered feedback to improve the system.

We presented our annotation system during ReAction day at Algonquin College. I created whiteboard art (above) and a poster (below) for our showcase.

Charley Harper

Graphic Design

The famous Charley Harper. If you aren't familiar with his work, I suggest having a quick Google. This project was 100% inspired by his work and focussed on basic gestalt principles—proximity, continuation, closure, and negative/positive space.

The initial concept sketches—using Harper’s work as example and inspiration, I chose an animal (the Red Panda), gathered as much research as possible and began sketching ideas.

The final concept sketches—when I had an idea of the shape and form that I liked, I worked on creating a cleaner version of the sketch.

The end result—after scanning and cleaning up the sketch further in Photoshop, I brought it into Illustrator and began working with it. After many hours of revision, the project reached a satisfactory result.
